What is RobotRoom?

RobotRoom is a next-generation experiential kids RPG environment, where your kids will help robots solve problems. Think of it as an alternative to bounce rooms, kids museums, or gym classes. Here, children not only burn energy but also engage with reading, math, and interactive puzzles—guided by AI-powered robots!

  • Real, physical robots with sophisticated AI
  • Fun environment: slides, toys, and fun activities
  • Educational tasks: reading, math, dance, and more
