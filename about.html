About RobotRoom

RobotRoom is founded by a team of educators, engineers, and parents who envisioned a place where children can engage their minds and bodies simultaneously. Our robots are designed to be friendly companions, educators, and teammates, guiding children through fun activities that promote learning and creativity.

We believe in nurturing curiosity, encouraging collaboration, and providing an environment where kids can have fun, learn, and grow—all while interacting with cutting-edge technology.
